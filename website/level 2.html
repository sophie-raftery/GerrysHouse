LEVEL 2

THE KITCHEN

🍳

📋 MISSION BRIEF

Something smells fishy in the kitchen — and it's not just the lingering scent of last night's dinner. After your discoveries in the bedroom, a new trail of clues leads you into the heart of the house: the kitchen.

Jarry spends a lot of time in this room, cooking, pacing, and generally being suspicious. You'll need to time your movements carefully, duck behind the kitchen island, and search the cupboards without rattling the pots and pans.

The second vinyl record is hidden somewhere in this room. Find it, collect more evidence, and uncover why Jarry has been acting so strangely around meal times. The kitchen holds more than just food.

🎯 OBJECTIVES

  • Enter the kitchen without alerting Jarry
  • Search the kitchen cupboards for hidden evidence
  • Check the refrigerator for suspicious items
  • Find and collect the Michael Jackson vinyl record
  • Avoid Jarry's patrol route around the kitchen island
  • Exit the kitchen with all evidence intact

💡 TIPS & TRICKS

  • Jarry follows a fixed patrol route — learn the timing
  • Crouching behind the kitchen island gives you full cover
  • The vinyl record is hidden behind a loose tile near the sink
  • Don't open the cupboard with the noisy hinges near the door
  • The refrigerator light comes on when opened — wait for darkness
