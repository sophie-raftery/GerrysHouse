LEVEL 3

THE GARAGE

🔧

📋 MISSION BRIEF

You've made it through the bedroom and the kitchen, but the investigation is far from over. Your evidence trail leads into the dark, cluttered garage — a room full of locked boxes, old tools, and secrets that Jarry has been keeping for years.

The garage is Jarry's domain. He comes here often and knows every inch of it. Your stealth skills will be pushed to the limit as you navigate between workbenches and shelves, searching for the final vinyl record and, more importantly, the key to the basement.

The truth about Patrick is locked behind the basement door. All the evidence gathered so far points here. Find the key, collect the last vinyl, and prepare yourself for what waits below.

🗺️ LEVEL MAP

🎯 OBJECTIVES

  • Enter the garage without triggering the motion sensor
  • Search the workbench drawers for tools and clues
  • Find and collect the Billy Joel vinyl record
  • Locate the locked box behind the shelving unit
  • Find the key to the basement door
  • Avoid being caught by Jarry in the garage
  • Exit the garage with the basement key secured

💡 TIPS & TRICKS

  • The motion sensor near the entrance can be bypassed by crouching
  • Jarry patrols the garage perimeter every 45 seconds
  • The vinyl record is tucked behind the paint cans on the back shelf
  • The basement key is inside the locked toolbox — find the combination
  • Knock something over to distract Jarry and create a window to move
