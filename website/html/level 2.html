LEVEL 2

THE KITCHEN

🍳

📋 MISSION BRIEF

Something smells fishy in the kitchen. After your discoveries in the bedroom, a new trail of clues leads you into the heart of the house, the kitchen.

Jarry's mother spends a lot of time in this room cooking. You'll need to time your movements carefully, duck behind the kitchen island, and search for the vinyl without rattling the pots and pans.

The second vinyl record is hidden somewhere in this room. Find it and collect more evidence to progress to the next level.

🗺️ LEVEL MAP

🎯 OBJECTIVES

  • Enter the kitchen without alerting anyone
  • Search the kitchen for hidden evidence
  • Find and collect the Billy Joel vinyl record
  • Avoid Jarry's mother's patrol route around the kitchen island
  • Exit the kitchen with all the evidence

💡 TIPS & TRICKS

  • Jarry's mother follows a fixed patrol route, learn the timing
  • Using the kitchen island lets you manoeuvre around her.
