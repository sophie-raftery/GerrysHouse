🏠 ABOUT THE GAME

Jarry's House is a stealth and investigation game built with Python and Pygame. The game takes place entirely inside one house and challenges the player to uncover a mystery through careful exploration, stealth and evidence gathering.

The player takes on the role of a secret investigator who has snuck into the home of Jarry. Your beloved family fish named Patrick has gone missing, and all signs point to Jarry. But the story is more layered than it first appears and as you move through each room, you begin to understand that this house has many secrets.

Each of the three levels - the Bedroom, the Kitchen and the Garage contains unique stealth challenges, collectibles, and narrative clues. Players must avoid Jarry's line of sight, solve puzzles, and collect three special vinyl records to unlock the truth.

The game was designed and built as a team project, combining programming, pixel art, level design, and narrative writing to create a cohesive indie game experience.
