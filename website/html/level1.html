LEVEL 1

THE BEDROOM

🛏️

📋 MISSION BRIEF

Your investigation begins in Jarry's bedroom — the most personal space in the house. Patrick, Jarry's family fish, has vanished under mysterious circumstances, and this room holds the first fragments of the truth.

Sneak through the bedroom without waking Jarry. Search the drawers, check under the bed, and look behind the wardrobe. There's something hidden here that Jarry doesn't want anyone to find — and it's your job to find it first.

Watch out for creaky floorboards. Jarry has a habit of waking up at the slightest sound. Keep your movements quiet, your eyes sharp, and leave no stone unturned.

🎯 OBJECTIVES

  • Enter the bedroom without being detected by Jarry
  • Search Jarry's bedroom for clues
  • Check for hidden items
  • Complete challenges
  • Find and collect the first evidence item
  • Exit the bedroom before Jarry arrives

💡 TIPS & TRICKS

  • Look for anything that seems out of place or glowing
