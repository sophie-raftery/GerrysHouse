📋 MISSION BRIEFING

Welcome to Jarry's House — a top-down stealth and investigation game set entirely inside one mysterious home. You play as a sneaky investigator trying to uncover the truth behind the disappearance of Patrick, a beloved pet fish who has gone missing under very suspicious circumstances.

Navigate through rooms filled with clues, hidden secrets, and a surprisingly suspicious homeowner named Jarry. Each room in the house contains a new puzzle, a new layer of the mystery, and new evidence to collect. But be careful — make too much noise and Jarry will know you're snooping around.

Collect vinyl records scattered throughout the house, avoid detection, and piece together what really happened in the basement. The truth is closer than you think — and stranger than you'd ever imagine. Can you solve the mystery of Jarry's House before it's too late?
